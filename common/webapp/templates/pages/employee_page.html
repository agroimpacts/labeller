Employee Center

From the Employee menu, you can:

  • For new employees:
    • View the training page to watch training videos
    • View the qualification page to pass the qualification test
  • For trained employees:
    • View the assignment page to map agricultural fields
    • View your assignment history page to see your success rate and send queries to the researchers

Hover over the Employee menu item above, and select the action desired from the sub-menu that appears.
