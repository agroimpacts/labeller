Using Active Learning To Map Agricultural Fields in Africa

Project Vision

To create a next-generation, open source, land cover mapping platform that optimally applies human judgement to guide machine learning, and can harness the deluge of data collected by rapidly expanding commercial satellite fleets, as well as the deep archives of legacy missions. We will use our platform to develop the next generation of agricultural maps for Sub-Saharan Africa (a region notoriously difficult to map), guided by the judgement of mappers who live in the regionSSA. Our maps will not only distinguish agricultural from non-agricultural areas with unprecedented accuracy, but will go beyond pixel-based classifications to map individual fields. This combined human-machine approach will overcome many current obstacles to mapping complex agricultural landscapes, and will approach the limits of detail and accuracy that can be achieved with satellite-based mapping.
